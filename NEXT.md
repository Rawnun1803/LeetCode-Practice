## Recommendation

* Aim for **100 problems with pattern recognition**, not 100 random problems.
* Maintain a GitHub repository with approach and complexity.
* Solve each problem yourself before viewing solutions.
* Revisit starred (★) problems after a week.

**By problem #100, you'll have covered around 80–90% of the patterns commonly asked in OA rounds and interviews.**
